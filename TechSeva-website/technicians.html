Frequently Asked Questions

Joining TechSeva is completely free. We only charge a service fee (10-15%) when you complete a job through our platform. There are no upfront costs or monthly fees.

You'll need to provide:

  • Government ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Proof of address
  • Any trade certificates or licenses you have
  • Bank account details for payments

Payments are processed within 24 hours after job completion. You can withdraw your earnings directly to your bank account every week. There's no need to collect cash from customers unless they prefer to pay that way (you'll still need to generate a digital receipt).

While our app provides the best experience, we also support SMS-based job alerts and IVR systems for technicians without smartphones. However, we recommend getting a basic Android smartphone to access all features.

Customers rate your service from 1-5 stars after each job. Your overall rating is the average of your last 50 jobs. Higher-rated technicians get priority for better jobs and can charge slightly higher rates.
